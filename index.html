Welcome to Deebass

Deebass is a company that provides rehearsal space including basic music instruments. We also hire out P.A systems for all events, weddings, church gatherings, funerals, corporate events, etc. We have been in the industry for 9 years and our goal is to provide artists with space where they are able to perfect their gifts and provide them with high quality sound.
